Events with our partners

(3:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m.)
3-4 p.m. ET

Transformative health policies for NCDs: Emphasizing the importance of an integrated approach for cardiorenal conditions

In partnership with Astrazeneca & Boehringer Ingelheim, ISN & Global Patient Alliance for Kidney Health

This event will deep-dive into the challenges and opportunities surrounding cardiovascular, renal and metabolic conditions, including the urgent need for global recognition of their close interconnections and for prioritizing their care.

Learn more about the event here.
4:30-6 p.m. ET

Ensuring cancer care for all: Tackling persisting health inequities

In partnership with MSD and American Cancer Society

This event will explore what’s needed to advance equitable and high-quality cancer care for all, as well as current obstacles to accessing cancer prevention, diagnosis, and treatment.

Learn more about the event here.
6:30-8:30 p.m. ET

Securing the Future: How humanitarian and development finance can foster peace and drive resilience in Africa

In partnership with the African Development Bank Group and UNHCR

This high-level event will discuss solutions for peace, security, and resilience in Africa over the past decade, with a focus on the African Development Fund’s 17th replenishment and integrating UNHCR priorities and partnerships to address forced displacement.

Learn more about the event here.
